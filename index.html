About Me

Hi there! My name is Jan, and I'm originally from the Czech Republic. Currently, I'm based in the beautiful city of Barcelona, where I'm pursuing my passion for technology.

After spending several years in sales and account management, I realised that I wanted to shift my career focus to technology. I've always been curious about how things work, and I wanted to gain a deeper understanding of the technology around me.

I graduated from 6-month intensive bootcamp with Le Wagon where I gained expertise in web development and programming. I learned how to build full-stack web applications using Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I also developed an understanding of programming concepts such as object-oriented programming, MVC architecture, and database design.

In my free time I love reading, playing footvolley, hiking and discovering new coffee places.
